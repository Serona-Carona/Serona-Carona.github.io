SYMPTOMS

Common signs of infection include respiratory symptoms, fever, cough, shortness of breath and breathing difficulties. In more severe cases, infection can cause pneumonia, severe acute respiratory syndrome, kidney failure and even death.

According to the information published by the Chinese health department, the clinical manifestations of the known cases of new coronavirus infection are fever, limb weakness, respiratory symptoms mainly with dry cough, and some people may have difficulty breathing. In severe cases, it may progress to severe pneumonia, respiratory distress syndrome or multiple organ failure, shock, and so on. According to the current epidemiological information, most of the patients can recover, but there are also deaths. Most of the deaths have a potential medical history, such as diabetes, chronic liver disease, renal insufficiency, and cardiovascular disease.
